U.S. State IQ and Well-Being Rankings

This visualization presents U.S. state rankings in IQ, well-being, crime, education, health, and income. The data was slightly adapted from a research article titled Updated IQ and Well-Being Scores for the 50 U.S. States, published in 2022 by the National Center for Biotechnology Information. The source code and data used for this visualization can be found on GitHub.
